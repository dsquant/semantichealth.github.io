Data Policy

Collection

We collect data from the form's required fields—state, age, zip code, health conditions. We also collect the list of plans served to you, and the ones you clicked on.

Usage

We use your data to improve the ranking of insurance plans, in order to make the search results as tailored to you as possible.

Access

Only our team has access to your data, and we will not share any piece of it without your permission.

Expiration

We delete data older than 12 months. If you have questions about whether your data is in our system, send us an email and we'll let you know.

Consent

By using Semantic Health, you are consenting to our data policy. If any part of it is not acceptable to you, please contact us and we will do our best to accommodate your requests.

Team

We are students from UC Berkeley's Master of Information and Data Science program. Signing up for health insurance from the Affordable Care Act (Obamacare) is difficult, and finding plans with preferred providers or drugs that you depend on involves significant work and expertise. We built Semantic Health with the goal to substantially improve the Obamacare enrollment process.
